IDEATION: A shorter interactive web version of my publication Space R(Age). The idea is to get people to become interested in the subject matter and then hopefully become interested in looking at the book. The purpose is not to relay all the information from the publication, but instead to garner interest. A clean minimal style with small animations and clean typography will be consistent throughout the site, along with photos from Nasa.
